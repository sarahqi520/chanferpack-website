Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our turnkey liquid detergent packaging lines.

What is the typical lead time for a complete liquid detergent packaging line?+

Standard configurations ship in 45–60 days. Custom turnkey projects (water treatment + filling + packaging + utilities) typically take 75–90 days from order confirmation to factory acceptance test (FAT). We provide a detailed Gantt chart at the quotation stage.

Can your line handle both liquid detergent and dishwashing liquid?+

Yes. Our servo-filling systems are designed for low-to-medium viscosity liquids (1–5,000 cP), covering laundry detergent, dishwashing liquid, fabric softener, disinfectant, and shampoo. Quick-change parts allow product changeover in under 30 minutes.

What bottle sizes and production speeds do you offer?+

Bottle lines range from 1,000 to 30,000 BPH covering 100ml–5L containers. Pouch lines cover 30–60 packs per minute for 10ml–1L formats. Drum lines handle 100–1,200 drums per hour for 5L–30L jerry cans and pails. Speed depends on container size, viscosity, and automation level.

Do you provide installation and training at the customer site?+

Yes. Every turnkey project includes on-site installation, commissioning, and operator training. Our engineers typically spend 2–4 weeks at your factory, depending on line complexity. We also offer remote video commissioning for cost-effective alternatives.

What certifications do your machines comply with?+

CHANFER is ISO 9001:2015 certified. Our machines comply with CE (European Conformity), and we can provide UL/CSA-certified electrical panels for North American customers. We also support FAT protocols aligned with ASME BPE for hygienic applications.

How do you handle after-sales service and spare parts?+

We provide a 12-month warranty with lifetime technical support. Critical spare parts ship within 48 hours via DHL/FedEx from our Guangzhou warehouse. Our service team offers 24/7 video support, and we can dispatch engineers to most countries within 5–7 days.
