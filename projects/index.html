Project FAQs

Questions about our turnkey factory projects and global installations.

How many turnkey projects has CHANFER completed?+

CHANFER has delivered 200+ turnkey liquid detergent production lines across 60+ countries since 2011. Our project portfolio covers capacities from 500 L/day (entry-level) to 50,000 L/day (industrial scale).

Which countries do you have the most installations in?+

Our top markets are Nigeria, Egypt, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Indonesia, Vietnam, Mexico, Colombia, and Ethiopia. We have dedicated sales engineers speaking Arabic, French, Spanish, and Portuguese to support these regions.

Do you help with factory layout and utilities planning?+

Yes. Our engineering team provides 2D/3D factory layout design, including equipment positioning, pipe routing, electrical distribution, HVAC, and compressed air systems. We also calculate utility requirements (water, steam, power) and ROI projections.

Can you provide references from similar projects?+

Yes. Upon signing an NDA, we can connect you with existing customers in similar industries or regions. We also publish case studies on our website covering production capacity, challenges solved, and customer outcomes.

What is included in a turnkey project?+

A turnkey project includes: process design, equipment manufacturing, installation, commissioning, operator training, formula optimization support, 12-month warranty, and lifetime technical service. Optional add-ons include water treatment, effluent treatment, and building design consultancy.
