Why Partner with CHANFER

💰

Strong Commission Structure

Earn 5–8% on every confirmed order. On a $100,000 line, that's $5,000–$8,000 per sale. Our top agents close 8–15 deals per year — that's $40,000–$120,000 in annual commission.

🗺️

Exclusive Regional Territory

You get exclusivity in your market. We don't sell direct to your customers. If a customer in your territory contacts us, we redirect them to you. Exclusivity is contractually protected.

🏭

Factory Training in China

5-day factory training at our Guangzhou facility — product deep-dive, demonstration of 3 live lines, FAT process, and sales training. We cover hotel and internal transport. You cover your own airfare.

📊

Full Marketing Toolkit

Branded brochures in your language, demo videos, case studies from your region, email templates, and a dedicated landing page co-branded with your company. We create the assets; you close the deals.

🛠️

Technical Support to Win Deals

Our engineering team will join your video calls with prospects, answer technical RFQ questions within 24 hours, and provide factory proposals in your customer's language within 48 hours.

📦

Spare Parts Stocking Support

Consignment spare parts inventory for distributors (min. $15,000 stock): you stock the parts, we bill you only when sold. Allows you to offer 48-hour local parts availability — a huge competitive advantage.
