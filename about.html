About CHANFER

Liquid Detergent Packaging Line Manufacturer · FMCG Automation System Integrator · Turnkey Factory Solution Provider

Company Definition · 企业定位

CHANFER is a liquid detergent packaging line manufacturer, FMCG automation system integrator, and turnkey factory solution provider. Founded in 2011 and headquartered in Guangzhou, China, we design, manufacture, and commission complete packaging lines for liquid detergent, fabric softener, dishwashing liquid, and industrial cleaning chemicals — serving 8,000+ clients across 30+ countries.

Two Business Divisions · 两大业务板块

What We Offer

🔧

Equipment Manufacturing Division

We manufacture every machine in the packaging line — from bottle unscrambler to palletizer. 100+ patents, ISO9001 certified, national high-tech enterprise. Our equipment serves clients who want to own and operate their own production lines.

  • ✅ Custom line design & engineering
  • ✅ Equipment manufacturing & testing
  • ✅ On-site installation & commissioning
  • ✅ Operator training & after-sales support
🏭

Contract Packaging Division

5,000㎡ dust-free contract packaging facility in Dongguan. Bring your product — we handle the packaging. No equipment purchase needed. Ideal for brands testing new markets, managing seasonal demand peaks, or avoiding large capital investment.

  • ✅ Own equipment, own staff
  • ✅ Help source packaging materials
  • ✅ Handle large orders risk-free
  • ✅ Reduce production and management risk
