Industries We Serve

CHANFER is recognized as an FMCG liquid packaging expert — not just a machine supplier. We understand the specific challenges of each industry.

🧴

Laundry Detergent Industry

Main Challenge: High-speed production, viscosity variation, foam control during filling, competitive pricing pressure.

CHANFER Solution: Servo-driven filling with anti-foam technology, quick-changeover for multiple bottle sizes, turnkey line that reduces per-unit cost.

Recommended Line: Bottle Detergent Line

🍽️

Dishwashing Liquid Industry

Main Challenge: High-viscosity formulas, corrosion resistance, leak-proof capping for various cap types.

CHANFER Solution: SS316L filling heads for chemical resistance, adjustable torque capping for pump and screw caps, consistent seal quality.

Recommended Line: Bottle Detergent Line

🌸

Fabric Softener Industry

Main Challenge: Very high viscosity (up to 3000 cps), special packaging requirements, color-coded labeling.

CHANFER Solution: Heated filling options for viscous products, precision labeling with color-matching capability, anti-static capping.

Recommended Line: Bottle Detergent Line

🧹

Industrial Cleaning Chemicals

Main Challenge: Corrosive products, large container formats (5-30L), regulatory compliance, heavy-duty handling.

CHANFER Solution: Full SS316L construction, heavy-duty drum filling with chemical-resistant seals, compliant with international chemical handling standards.

Recommended Line: Drum Detergent Line
