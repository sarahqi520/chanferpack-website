Industry Application FAQs

Questions about how our machines adapt to different industry applications.

Which industries commonly use your packaging lines?+

Our primary markets are household cleaning (laundry detergent, dishwashing liquid, fabric softener), personal care (shampoo, body wash, hand soap), automotive chemicals, and agricultural chemicals. We also serve food-grade applications (cooking oil, sauces) with sanitary configurations.

Can your lines handle corrosive chemicals?+

Yes. For corrosive products (acids, alkalis, bleach), we offer PP/PVDF fluid contact parts and 316L stainless steel frames. Our chemical-resistant filling systems are widely used for sodium hypochlorite, hydrochloric acid, and industrial cleaners.

Do you have food-grade certified equipment?+

Yes. Our food-grade lines use 316L stainless steel, sanitary tri-clamp connections, and comply with FDA and EU food contact regulations. We provide material certificates and surface finish reports (Ra ≤ 0.8 μm) for hygienic applications.

What about explosive or flammable products?+

For flammable products (alcohol-based sanitizers, solvents), we offer ATEX/IECEx-certified explosion-proof configurations. This includes explosion-proof motors, intrinsically safe sensors, and nitrogen-purged electrical cabinets.

Can you customize for unusual container shapes?+

Yes. We have experience with oval bottles, square containers, asymmetrical jugs, and custom-molded PET. Our bottle unscrambler and orientation systems can be tailored to your specific container drawings. Mold fees may apply for highly custom designs.
