📐 Methodology & Assumptions

  • Annual production = BPH × utilization × 16 hours × 250 days
  • Annual savings = (outsource cost - in-house cost) × annual production
  • Payback period = equipment cost ÷ annual savings
  • 5-year NPV assumes 8% discount rate (conservative for emerging markets)
  • Maintenance + spares cost (3% of equipment cost) included in year-by-year projection
  • Installation + freight + duties not included in FOB price — add 30% for true landed cost
