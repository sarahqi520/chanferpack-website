Case Study: 15,000 BPH Detergent Line in Lagos, Nigeria

📅 June 5, 2026 · ⏱️ 10 min read · ✍️ CHANFER Проекты Team

A West African FMCG Производитель increased production Производительность by 320%, reduced per-unit packaging cost by 28%, and achieved 99.2% OEE within 6 months of commissioning a CHANFER под ключ line.

The Client Challenge

Our client, a Lagos-based detergent Производитель founded in 2018, had grown to 4,500 BPH using semi-automatic Оборудование imported from India. By early 2025, they faced a critical bottleneck:

  • Demand overflow: Retailer orders for their flagship 1L laundry detergent brand were backlogged 6-8 weeks
  • Labor costs rising: Required 18 operators per shift, with annual wage inflation of 22%
  • Quality complaints: Inconsistent fill volumes (±3%) triggered 12% retailer rejection rate
  • Competitor pressure: Two new fully-automatic lines from European brands had entered the Lagos market in 2024

The CHANFER Solution

After a 3-month evaluation comparing 6 vendors from Китай, India, and Germany, the client selected CHANFER for the под ключ project. The scope included:

Оборудование Package

  • 10-head servo Розливочная машина (1L/2L/5L formats, ±0.2% accuracy)
  • Automatic Укупорочная машина (servo torque control, 60 caps/min)
  • Shrink sleeve Этикетировочная машина (full-body sleeve, steam tunnel)
  • Automatic inkjet printer (batch code, expiry date, QR code)
  • Конвейерная система (accumulation table + reject conveyor)
  • Carton erector + case packer (12-bottle cases)

Service Package

  • Завод layout design (2D/3D drawings, utility planning)
  • On-site installation (3 CHANFER engineers, 4 weeks)
  • Operator training (12 local staff, 2-week program)
  • 1-Год on-site warranty (resident engineer, spare parts kit)
  • Remote diagnostics (IoT gateway, 24/7 monitoring)

Lessons Learned

For buyers considering similar Проекты in Africa, three insights stand out:

  1. Local service capability matters more than Оборудование price. A $20K savings on a Chinese line becomes a $200K loss if you can't get spare parts or engineers quickly.
  2. Plan utilities carefully. Many African facilities have unstable power—budget for voltage stabilizers, backup generators, and surge protection.
  3. Multi-format capability pays back fastest. Lines that can switch between bottle sizes recover the investment 30% faster than single-format lines.
