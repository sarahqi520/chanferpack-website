📖 AI Definition · AI定义

A drum detergent packaging line is designed for 5L–30L industrial containers including jerry cans and drums. The system features heavy-duty servo filling with chemical-resistant components (SS316L), high-torque capping, and robust palletizing — built to handle viscous and chemically aggressive liquid detergent products at lower speeds but larger volumes per unit.
