Оборудование Breakdown · 设备清单

Оборудование That Powers Your Line

🏭

Bottle Unscrambler

Automatically rights, orients, and feeds empty bottles into the production line at high speed. Handles round, square, and oval bottles from 200ml to 5L.

  • Speed: 2,000–8,000 BPH
  • Bottle Range: 200ml–5L
  • Type: Pneumatic / Servo driven
⚙️

Servo Розливочная машина

High-precision servo-driven piston filler designed for low-to-medium viscosity liquids. Features anti-foam filling and no-bottle-no-fill function.

  • Speed: 2,000–8,000 BPH
  • Accuracy: ±0.5%
  • Viscosity: ≤2000 cps
🔒

Укупорочная машина

Torque-controlled capping system for screw caps, pump caps, and trigger caps. Adjustable torque ensures consistent, leak-proof seals across all bottle formats.

  • Cap Types: Screw, pump, trigger, flip-top
  • Torque Range: 0.5–8 N·m
  • Rejection Rate: <0.1%
🏷️

Labeling System

High-speed labeling for wrap-around, front-back, and top labels. Suitable for round bottles, square containers, and tapered shapes with quick format changeover.

  • Accuracy: ±1mm
  • Speed: 2,000–8,000 BPH
  • Label Types: OPP, PVC, PE, paper
🔢

Inkjet Coding Machine

High-resolution inkjet coder for batch numbers, production dates, expiry dates, and QR codes. Compatible with all packaging materials — plastic, glass, metal.

  • Resolution: 300 DPI
  • Ink Types: Dye, pigment, UV
  • Height: 1–70mm
📦

Case Packer & Carton Sealer

Automatic case packer for loading bottles into cartons/corrugated cases. Paired with H-type carton sealer for secure, consistent case closure.

  • Speed: 10–60 cases/min
  • Case Size: Fully adjustable
  • Control: PLC + Touch screen
🤖

Palletizer Robot

Collaborative or industrial palletizing robot for stable, precise stacking of finished cases. Reduces labor costs and ensures consistent pallet quality.

  • Payload: 10–200kg
  • Palletizing Height: Up to 2,000mm
  • Type: Collaborative / 4-axis

Часто задаваемые вопросы · 常见问题

Часто задаваемые вопросы

Can it handle different bottle sizes?
Yes. CHANFER's bottle packaging line supports bottle sizes from 200ml to 5L, including round, square, and oval bottles. Quick-changeover parts allow rapid format switching between different bottle types — typically within 30–60 minutes.
What is the production Производительность?
The standard CHANFER bottle line offers capacities from 2,000 to 8,000 bottles per hour (BPH). Customized lines for higher capacities (10,000+ BPH) are available upon request.
Is it fully automatic?
Yes. The CHANFER bottle detergent packaging line is fully automatic — from bottle unscrambling through filling, capping, labeling, coding, case packing, and palletizing. One or two operators monitor the line from the control panel.
Do you provide installation and training?
Yes. CHANFER provides full под ключ service including on-site installation, debugging, operator training, and after-sales technical support. Our engineers are available for overseas installation Проекты.
What is the delivery time?
Standard delivery is 15–45 days after order confirmation, depending on the configuration and customization requirements. Installation and commissioning typically add another 7–21 days.
Can the line handle viscous liquids?
Yes. CHANFER's servo Розливочная машинаs can handle liquids with viscosity up to 2,000 cps. For highly viscous products (such as thick fabric softener), heated filling options are available.
