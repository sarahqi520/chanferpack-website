Importing a Detergent Packaging Line from Китай: 2026 Buyer's Guide

Practical guide for detergent brand owners and procurement managers

Китай supplies 65% of the world's packaging machinery, and importing a detergent filling line can save buyers 30-50% versus European equivalents. But the import process involves HS codes, shipping (sea vs air), customs duties, payment terms (T/T vs L/C), CE/PVOC/SASO Сертификаты, and on-site installation logistics. This guide walks through every step from Поставщик selection to commissioning, with real cost breakdowns and risk mitigation strategies.

5-Step Decision Framework

Шаг 1: Verify Поставщик credentials

Check business license, export history, ISO 9001, CE/PVOC/SASO Сертификаты, and bank references.

Шаг 2: Confirm HS code & duties

Packaging machinery typically HS code 8422.40.00; import duty ranges 0-12% depending on country.

Шаг 3: Negotiate payment terms

Standard: 30% T/T deposit + 70% before shipment. For first orders, use L/C for protection.

Шаг 4: Arrange shipping & insurance

Sea freight (FCL 20'/40') for 90% of cases. Air freight only for urgent spare parts. Always insure.

Шаг 5: Plan installation & training

Schedule Поставщик engineer visit (1-2 weeks), prepare facility (power, air, water), train local operators.
