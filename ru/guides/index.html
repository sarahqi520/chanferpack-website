Industry Knowledge · 行业知识

FMCG Packaging Automation Trends 2025

1. Pouch Packaging Growth: Refill pouches are growing 15–20% annually in emerging markets (Africa, Southeast Asia, South Asia). Lower material cost + eco narrative = strong consumer appeal.

2. Robotics & Collaborative Automation: Collaborative palletizers (cobot palletizers) are replacing traditional industrial robots in SME упаковочных линий — lower cost, easier programming, smaller footprint.

3. Smart Завод Integration: IoT-enabled упаковочных линий with real-time OEE monitoring, predictive maintenance alerts, and remote diagnostics are becoming standard in new installations.

4. Sustainability Regulation: UAE, Saudi Arabia, and EU markets are implementing stricter plastic reduction regulations. Flexible packaging (pouches) and concentrated product formats are gaining regulatory approval faster than rigid bottles.
